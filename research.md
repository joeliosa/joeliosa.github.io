<h1>Research</h1>

<p>My PhD work is situated at IRIT Toulouse under the supervision of <a href="#">Dr. Emiliano Lorini</a>. I work on formal foundations for AI alignment, with a focus on multi-agent systems, responsibility attribution, and the logic of collective behavior.</p>
