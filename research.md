<h2>Interests</h2>

<p>Beyond my core PhD work, I am drawn to questions at the boundary of formal theory and human systems: mechanism design as applied to institutional coordination, values elicitation and preference aggregation, and the political philosophy of emerging AI governance. I am particularly interested in how tools from logic and learning theory can be made load-bearing in real organizations rather than remaining purely theoretical.</p>
